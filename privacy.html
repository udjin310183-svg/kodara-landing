Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kodara ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Kodara service ("Service").

1. What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account data — email address when you create an account or log in
  • Codebase metadata — file names, dependency graphs, module summaries generated by kodara init. We do not collect your source code.
  • Usage data — anonymous query counts and token savings statistics stored locally on your machine
  • Payment data — handled entirely by our payment processor (Paddle). We do not store card numbers or payment details.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • Your source code or file contents
  • Personal data beyond your email address
  • Browsing history or tracking cookies

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your account and verify your subscription
  • To store and restore your project index backups (if you use cloud backup)
  • To send transactional emails (login codes, receipts)

4. Data Storage

Account data and cloud backups are stored in Supabase (EU region). Payment processing is handled by Paddle. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or your subscription lapses, your cloud data is deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at support@getkodara.dev.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS, row-level security in our database, and email OTP authentication. No passwords are stored.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@getkodara.dev.
